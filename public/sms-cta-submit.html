SMS opt-in consent submission

For businesses documenting customer SMS consent (A2P / carrier review). Required fields include business and customer contact information. The opt-in checkbox is not pre-selected.

Submitting sends a copy of this record and a PDF to admin@tradesman-us.com for Tradesman compliance archives. You should still record the same consent inside your Tradesman account under Customers → SMS opt-in before sending texts from the platform.
